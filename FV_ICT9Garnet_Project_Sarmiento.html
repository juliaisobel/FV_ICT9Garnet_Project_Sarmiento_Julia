🔥 HOW TO PREVENT FIRE IN YOUR HOME 🏡


💥If your home has a fireplace, do not leave a fire in a fireplace unattended and just use it whenever it is needed only. Children should also be kept away from the fireplace since it is dangerous. Make sure to extinguish the fire first before leaving the fireplace.

💥If you have a heater at home, make sure there is space from the items around it. Make sure there are no flammable things near it such as paper, clothes, carpets, rugs or other flammable liquids. Make sure as well that children can't reach the heater so easily.

💥Whenever your house experiences a power outrage, make sure you and your family use flashlights instead of candles. It is safer to use flashlights than candles to avoid occuring fire. It is not safe to use candles especially in the dark since there is a high chance that a person might accidentally hit it and cause fire.
